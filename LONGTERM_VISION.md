# Long-Term Vision — AI Solutions Architect & Independent Consultant

---

## The Goal

> "Give me any business problem → I design + build the AI solution → deploy it → you pay me → I maintain the infra."

A **one-person AI agency** — someone who can walk into any company, understand their problem, and build the AI solution end-to-end. No dependency on a team. No waiting for approvals. Just problem → solution → delivery.

**Market titles for this role:**
- AI Solutions Architect
- Freelance AI Consultant
- Independent AI Engineer
- AI Automation Specialist

## The Domain Differentiator — ERP + AI (All Platforms)

Most AI engineers don't understand ERP. Most ERP consultants don't understand AI.
**You will be the bridge — across all ERP platforms, not just SAP.** That's a rare, high-value position.

### ERP Landscape to Cover

| ERP Platform | Market | AI Opportunity |
|-------------|--------|----------------|
| **SAP** (S/4HANA, BTP, Joule) | Large enterprise, manufacturing, finance | SAP AI Core, Joule extensions, BTP integrations |
| **Oracle ERP / Oracle Fusion** | Large enterprise, finance, HR | Oracle AI Services, OCI, autonomous features |
| **Microsoft Dynamics 365** | Mid-market, SMB | Azure OpenAI integration, Copilot for D365 |
| **Salesforce** | CRM + ERP crossover | Einstein AI, Agentforce, Flow automation |
| **NetSuite** | SMB, fast-growing companies | AI-powered workflows, SuiteScript automation |
| **Odoo** | SMB, open source | Python-based, easiest to extend with custom AI |
| **Workday** | HR + Finance | Workday AI, Extend platform |

### Start with SAP (your current org advantage), then expand:
- **Year 1:** SAP — because you're already inside SAP SE, use that access
- **Year 2:** Microsoft Dynamics 365 — largest mid-market share, Azure AI is mature
- **Year 2+:** Salesforce / Oracle — as client demand grows

### Key AI integration patterns across ALL ERPs:
- **RAG over ERP data** — answer business questions from ERP documents, reports, manuals
- **AI agents that call ERP APIs** — automate data entry, approvals, reconciliation
- **Anomaly detection** — flag unusual transactions, fraud, data quality issues
- **Forecasting** — demand planning, cash flow, inventory on top of ERP data
- **Document processing** — invoices, purchase orders, contracts → structured ERP data
- **Chatbots over ERP** — "What's my current inventory for product X?" answered by an AI agent

### SAP-Specific AI (priority — your current employer)
- **SAP BTP (Business Technology Platform)** — where SAP AI lives
- **SAP AI Core** — deploy and serve ML models inside SAP
- **SAP AI Launchpad** — manage AI workflows
- **Joule (SAP's AI copilot)** — understand how it works and how to extend it
- **SAP Integration Suite** — connect AI agents to SAP systems via APIs

## What a Client Engagement Looks Like (Future State)

1. **Discovery** — Client describes their problem (manual process, data buried in documents, slow decisions, no automation)
2. **Scoping** — You identify the right AI pattern (RAG? Agent? Fine-tune? Simple automation?)
3. **Proposal** — You write a 1-page solution summary + timeline + price
4. **Build** — You build it: Python + FastAPI + LLM + vector DB + Docker + Cloud
5. **Deploy** — You push it to Cloud Run / AWS / Azure — live URL, working system
6. **Handover + Maintenance** — You document it, train the client, offer a monthly retainer for maintenance
